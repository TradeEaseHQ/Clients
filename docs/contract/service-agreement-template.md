## 1. Services Included

The monthly fee covers the following services for Client's business:

- **Custom website** — professionally designed, hosted on Vercel with Client's domain
- **Local SEO optimization** — schema.org markup, meta descriptions, Open Graph tags, mobile viewport, page speed optimization
- **AI virtual assistant widget** — chat-style widget on the website, powered by AI, configured with Client's business information
- **Quote request form** — embedded contact form for client lead capture
- **Mobile-optimized design** — tested on mobile, tablet, and desktop screen sizes
- **Reasonable change requests** — updates to text content, photos, services list, business hours, or contact information (see Section 6)

---

## 2. Services Not Included

The following are not included under the monthly fee and will be quoted separately if requested:

- Paid advertising (Google Ads, Facebook Ads, etc.)
- Social media management or content creation
- Review automation or reputation management tools
- Online booking or scheduling integration
- Full site redesigns outside the current template
- Integrations with third-party CRMs or booking platforms

These may be offered as add-ons in a future package upgrade.

## 3. Fees and Billing

- Monthly fee is as stated above, billed on the date specified.
- Payment is due within 30 days of the billing date.
- If payment is not received within 30 days of due date, the site will be suspended (taken offline) until payment is made. The site will not be deleted.
- The site will be reinstated within 24 hours of confirmed payment.
- Pricing is locked for the first 6 months. Provider reserves the right to adjust pricing with 30 days written notice after that period.

---

## 4. Intellectual Property

- Provider owns all website code, design, and associated assets during the active relationship.
- Client owns their domain name at all times. Provider will never take custody of Client's domain registrar credentials.
- Upon cancellation of this agreement, Client will receive a full HTML export of their website within 5 business days, at no charge. Client may host this export independently.

---

## 5. Cancellation

- Either party may cancel this agreement with 30 days written notice (email is acceptable).
- The final month's fee is non-refundable regardless of cancellation timing.
- The website will remain live through the end of the paid period, then be taken offline.
- Client will receive an HTML export of their site as described in Section 4.

---

## 6. Change Requests

- Reasonable change requests are covered under the monthly fee at no additional charge. This includes updates to: business name or contact info, service descriptions, service areas, business hours, photos (provided by Client), FAQ items, and quote form fields.
- "Reasonable" is defined as changes that take less than 2 hours of work.
- Target turnaround for change requests: same business day, received by 3pm local time.
- Full redesigns, new pages, or custom development work outside the existing template will be scoped and quoted separately before any work begins.
