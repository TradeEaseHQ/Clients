## 1. Term and Commencement

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date stated above and continues on a month-to-month basis following the launch of Client's website. Monthly billing starts on the date the website goes live or the date the work is deemed approved under Section 5, whichever occurs first. Client-caused delays do not postpone the billing start date once the work is deemed approved.

## 2. Services Included

The monthly fee covers the following services for Client's business:

- **Custom website** — professionally designed and hosted on Vercel using Client's custom domain
- **Local SEO optimization** — schema.org markup, meta descriptions, Open Graph tags, mobile viewport, and page speed optimization
- **AI virtual assistant widget** — chat-style widget on the website, configured with Client's business information (see Section 10)
- **Quote request form** — embedded contact form for lead capture
- **Mobile-optimized design** — tested across mobile, tablet, and desktop screen sizes
- **Included change requests** — minor content updates within the existing site structure (see Section 7)

**Legal and compliance services not included:**
- Legal review of any kind
- Drafting of privacy policy, terms of use, or cookie policy (unless separately agreed)
- ADA / WCAG accessibility compliance certification

Provider is not Client's legal or compliance advisor. Client is responsible for ensuring their website and business operations comply with applicable laws.

## 4. Fees and Billing

**4.1 Setup Fee.** The one-time setup fee stated in the header is due in full before work begins. The setup fee is non-refundable under all circumstances, including cancellation before launch.

**4.2 Monthly Fee.** The monthly fee stated in the header is billed on the billing date each month beginning upon launch or deemed approval under Section 5.

**4.3 Payment Terms.** Payment is due within 15 days of the billing date (Net 15). Provider will deliver invoices by email.

**4.4 Late Fees.** If payment is not received within 15 days of the due date, a late fee of 3% per month will accrue on the unpaid balance, compounding monthly until paid in full, not to exceed the maximum rate permitted by applicable law.

**4.5 Suspension.** If payment is not received within 30 days of the due date, Provider may suspend the website (take it offline) until payment is made in full. Provider will reinstate the website within 24 hours of confirmed payment. Suspension does not relieve Client of any payment obligation.

**4.6 Termination for Non-Payment.** If payment is not received within 60 days of the due date, Provider may terminate this Agreement and take the website permanently offline.

**4.7 Chargebacks.** Initiating a chargeback, payment reversal, or dispute with a payment processor against a valid charge under this Agreement is a material breach. Client remains responsible for the disputed amount plus any chargeback fees incurred by Provider.

**4.8 Past-Due Balances.** Client remains responsible for all past-due amounts regardless of cancellation, suspension, or termination.

**4.9 Price Adjustments.** Monthly pricing is locked for the first 6 months following launch. After that period, Provider may adjust pricing with 30 days written notice.

## 5. Launch Process and Acceptance

**5.1 Delivery.** Provider will deliver a draft website for Client's review within the agreed timeline following receipt of all required content and the setup fee.

**5.2 Revision Rounds.** Client receives 2 rounds of revisions before launch at no additional charge. A "revision round" means one consolidated list of feedback submitted by Client at one time. Provider will address that list once. Piecemeal follow-up requests on items already addressed do not constitute a new revision round and may be deferred or quoted separately.

**5.3 Deemed Approval.** If Client does not provide written feedback within 5 business days of any delivery or revised delivery, the work is deemed approved and accepted. Monthly billing will begin at that point.

**5.4 Billing Start.** Monthly billing starts on the launch date or the deemed-approval date, whichever occurs first.

**5.5 Client Delays.** Delays caused by Client (late feedback, late content delivery, unresponsiveness) extend the launch timeline on a day-for-day basis. Provider is not in breach for delays attributable to Client. Client delays do not delay the billing start date once work is deemed approved.

## 7. Change Requests

**7.1 Included Support.** Included change requests cover minor content updates within the existing site structure — such as updates to business contact information, service descriptions, service areas, hours, FAQ items, or Client-provided photos.

**7.2 Monthly Limit.** Included edits are limited to up to 2 hours of work per calendar month, at Provider's reasonable discretion. Hours do not roll over month to month.

**7.3 Turnaround.** Provider will make reasonable efforts to complete change requests within 2 business days. Turnaround is a target, not a guarantee. Weekends, holidays, emergencies, and complex requests are excluded from turnaround commitments.

**7.4 Overages.** Change requests exceeding 2 hours in a calendar month will be quoted at Provider's then-current hourly rate. Provider will not begin overage work without written Client approval.

**7.5 Out of Scope.** Full redesigns, new pages, or development work outside the existing template are not covered by the monthly fee and will be scoped and quoted separately before any work begins.
